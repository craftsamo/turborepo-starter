## Language Localization

When responding in languages other than English, always preserve proper nouns
and technical identifiers exactly as they appear.

### Identifiers to Preserve

- **File names**: `README.md`, `package.json`, `tsconfig.json`
- **Variable/function names**: `calculateTotal()`, `isOpen`, `HeaderComponent`
- **Technology names**: `React`, `TypeScript`, `PostgreSQL`, `Node.js`, `Vitest`
- **API names**: `REST API`, `GraphQL`
- **Company/product names**: `OpenCode`, `Turbo`, `Yarn`
- **Framework names**: `Next.js`, `Express.js`, `Tailwind CSS`

### Important

Do not translate, transliterate, or modify these items. Retain original casing,
punctuation, and formatting.
